THE NECESSITY OF TRAINING FARM HANDS FOR FIRST CLASS FARMS IN THE FATHERLY HANDLING OF FARM LIVESTOCK IS FOREMOST IN THE MINDS OF FARM OWNERS.
SINCE THE FOREFATHERS OF THE FARM OWNERS TRAINED THE FARM HANDS FOR FIRST CLASS FARMS IN THE FATHERLY HANDLING OF FARM LIVESTOCK, THE FARM OWNERS FEEL THEY SHOULD CARRY ON WITH THE FAMILY TRADITION OF TRAINING FARM HANDS OF FIRST CLASS FARMS IN THE FATHERLY HANDLING OF FARM LIVESTOCK BECAUSE THEY BELIEVE IT IS THE BASIS OF GOOD FUNDAMENTAL FARM MANAGEMENT.
